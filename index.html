Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 30, 2025

This privacy policy applies to the Deep Prompt extension (the "Extension").

1. Data Collection

The Extension does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. All processing happens locally on your machine. We do not have access to your prompts, history, or any other information you provide while using the Extension.

2. Third-Party Services

The Extension does not share data with any third-party services. Since no data is collected, no data is sold or distributed to third parties.

3. Permissions

The Extension only requests the minimum permissions necessary to function. Any permissions granted are used solely to facilitate the local features of the tool.

4. Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, you can open an issue on the GitHub repository for this project.
